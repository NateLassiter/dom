Steve Aoki: TomorrowWorld 2013 now playing

Relive The Experience

Yesterday is History, Today is a Gift, Tomorrow is Mystery

Arriving at TomorrowWorld, we see a 50-foot sign rising before us, with the words...

“This Is Your Life. Make Every Second Legendary.”

The sentiment pulsed throughout the weekend, reminding everyone it’s our job to make life legendary . . . through music, through people, through experiences.

Three days, eight electrifying stages, more than 230 DJs from around the world, and 140,000 pulsing fans of dance music.

Ten-foot rainbow colored mushrooms, food courts shaped like green lily pads, and wide-eyed fish spitting fire from a calm lake added to the experience, immersing us in the cosmos that is TomorrowWorld.

Undoubtedly a legendary weekend.

Day and night, energy rippled non-stop through the crowd. Dancing skin to skin, swirling with eyes to the sky, sitting on shoulders with hands raised, bouncing foot to foot, fist pumping in sync with the pulse of the beat.

More than a song title or words on a t-shirt...

Eat, Sleep, Rave, Repeat became the lifestyle of the weekend.

A surreal feeling washes over us watching everyone dressed in costumes, dancing with smiles as the sun sets yellow and orange in the sky.

Dance music fans traveled from all over the world to Chattahoochee Hills, Georgia for TomorrowWorld. Over 50 nationalities attended, and for some it was their introduction to the U.S. All embraced one another as friends, high fiving, hugging, and dancing together.

8 massive stages, each a marvel in its own right.

Gigantic rotating LED screens, flame throwers, 20-foot high water fountains, firework platforms, smoke machines, lazers covering every color of the rainbow, candy land like decorations together creating the feel of the original TomorrowLand in Belgium. The main stage towered 400 feet, a masterpiece that appeared as if out of a fairy tale.

The sound system's pulsing base vibrates through our bodies and ensures that everyone dances in sync with each other and the music.

Fans at the sprawling, tree filled festival crossed bridges over a large pond to reach the stages. Along the way excitement ran so high fans continuously high-fived each other as they passed.

TomorrowWorld truly showcased the breadth of Electronic Dance Music.

From Deep House to Psychedelic Trance to Drum and Bass to Dirty Dubstep, all aspects of the genre were covered. Several DJs performed together to bring the crowd something never seen before. Afrojack and Steve Aoki teamed to create Afroki, and Steve Aoki, Laidback Luke, and Dimitri Vegas joined dressed as Superman, Batman, and Spiderman respectively.

“This Is Your Life. Make Every Second Legendary.”

We couldn’t agree more!!!
